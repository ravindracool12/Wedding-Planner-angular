Tips to hire hand-picked event management companies in Delhi

Professional event management companies provide event planning services which are inclusive of event management and coordination, décor, vendor management, catering, venue sourcing and organizing entertainment activities. Everything from scratch to end is handled by the experts. Check out the mentioned services in brief for a better understanding of the services.

Event planning and management: Professional experts possess the required skill set and framework to execute all the creative and logistical aspects. Irrespective of the event nature, experts have the skill to identify the audience preference and deliver the same.

Event coordination: Every little detail from lighting to food preparation is taken care of by the experts. Experts coordinate with multiple vendors simultaneously to deliver customer expectations.

Venue consultation: Venue is sourced considering the budget, locality preference, number of guests and other logistical arrangements like parking space etc.

Décor and other services: Décor, catering, lighting, entertainment activities etc. are planned and executed considering the event nature and customer preferences.

Event planner charges

Professional event management companies usually organize an event within the budget quoted by the customer. However, considering all the event requirements and considerations, experts will share a rough quotation of the estimated event charges. Be clear with the service provider about your event budget limit and the quality of services preferred.

Approximately, top professional experts will charge Rs 50,000- Rs1,50,000 for corporate event planning. Birthday party planning charges could range between Rs15,000- Rs.40,000. Event planning for wedding functions is expensive comparatively and can attract a cost of Rs.5,00,000- Rs.20,00,000 depending on the event preparations.

Qualities to look for in a professional event planner

Hiring the best of experts need not necessarily be experienced enough, other essential qualities matter too. Ensure that the shortlisted expert has the following skills mentioned below.

  • Professionalism:Event planning requires planning and managing multiple arrangements. Only a sort after professional will have efficient skills and clarity in planning and organizing.
  • Creativeness: The expert must be updated with the latest event trends and styles happening in the city. This makes the planning process easier and helps to host a memorable celebration.
  • Cordial and communicates efficiently: Having a cordial and effective communication is always helpful. Event planning is a tedious and time-consuming job. The expert must have patience and alertness to cater to all the event requirements to provide timely services.
